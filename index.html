Briefly about me

My journey in IT really started in 2016 when I began working as a tester for Apple products (iPhones, iPads) while I was still an university student. Aside from that, I worked as a Data analyst at The Confederation of industry of the Czech Republic.
The work was put at halt when I went to Japanese university thanks to an exchange programme.
---------------------------------
I got a master's degree at my university after I returned from the successful stay in Japan. I started working as a ministerial councillor at the Ministry of interior of the Czech Republic as soon as I got my diploma. Data anylysis is a large part of my work. I deal with several bilions of Czech crowns that are set to help fight serious crime and secure Czech (and European) borders.
---------------------------------
I deepened my SQL skills and learnt Java, Selenium, SoapUI, Tableau, and MS power BI basically from scratch during my free time. It is a way to keep myself constantly learning even outside my work.
